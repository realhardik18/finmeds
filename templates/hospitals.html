WHAT IT DOES

Introducing our innovative feature: a user-friendly search bar that allows you to effortlessly find hospitals and their policies. Upon entering a hospital name, key information—such as registration and treatment costs—will be clearly highlighted for easy reading. Each detail will be presented in a structured manner, ensuring you can quickly grasp essential points. For further information, direct links to the hospital's official website will be provided, offering a seamless way to access the most accurate and up-to-date details.
