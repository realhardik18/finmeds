Why We Do It

We aim to tackle the lack of transparency in healthcare costs, as many people are unaware of the true regulations associated with treatments and surgeries. Our mission is to spread awareness about hospital policies, empowering patients to make informed decisions and fostering trust in their healthcare providers.

How We Do It

We gather data from the publicly accessible websites of hospitals and healthcare services, specifically focusing on their terms and policies regarding procedures. This information allows us to help you to provide an accurate estimate of your medical bills before you undergo any procedures. By doing so, we aim to prevent any unexpected discrepancies between the estimated costs and the actual hospital bills you may receive. This proactive approach not only helps you avoid financial surprises but also empowers you to challenge any unfair billing practices that may arise. Ultimately, our goal is to help you save a significant amount of money while ensuring that you are fully informed about your healthcare expenses.
